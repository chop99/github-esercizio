Focaccia whit Caramelize Onions, Craberries, end Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped whith sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4cups (500g)all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teapsoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onios thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired,though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or fronzen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sezed mixing bowl,or in a strand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix togher all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the dough,thougt I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to icorporate the last of the flour...
